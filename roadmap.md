# Helin Tunalı – 24-Month Performance Marketing Roadmap

## Phase 1 (0–6 Months) – Technical Foundations
- Meta Ads mastery: structure, bidding, A/B tests
- Google Ads: Search + PMax fundamentals
- GA4 interpretation, Looker dashboards
- Weekly: performance deep dive + reporting practice

## Phase 2 (6–12 Months) – Strategy & Funnel Thinking
- Persona-based audience planning
- Full-funnel strategy design (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU)
- Creative insights → creative briefs
- Segment-based media plans
- Monthly: mini case study + test planning

## Phase 3 (12–24 Months) – Growth & Attribution
- CRM-linked performance optimization
- Offline conversion import workflows
- Cohort, LTV analysis basics
- Budget reallocation frameworks
- Client-facing presentation mastery
- Quarterly: growth experiments

## Success Indicators
- Month 6 → Full-funnel specialist seviyesine yaklaşma  
- Month 12 → Medium-level PM: Strategy + execution together  
- Month 24 → Senior PM / Growth Specialist yaklaşımı
