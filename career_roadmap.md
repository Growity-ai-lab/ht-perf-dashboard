# Helin Tunalı – 24-Month Performance Marketing Career Roadmap

This document describes a suggested 24-month roadmap for Helin to grow from **junior biddable / performance executive** to **senior performance / growth specialist**.

---

## Phase 1 – Technical Deepening & Foundations (Months 0–6)

**Main goal:** Become a solid **Performance Marketing Specialist** with strong platform knowledge and clean execution.

### Focus Areas

1. **Meta & Google Ads Mastery (Core Level)**
   - Meta:
     - Campaign structures (BAU vs always-on vs burst campaigns)
     - Advantage+ setups, CBO vs ABO
     - Bid strategies: lowest cost vs tCPA vs tROAS
     - Event structures and pixel/conversions API basics
   - Google Ads:
     - Search, Performance Max, basic YouTube campaigns
     - Match types, negative keywords, responsive search ads
     - PMax: asset groups, audience signals, basic feed optimization

2. **Measurement & Analytics Basics**
   - Read and interpret:
     - CTR, CPC, CPM, CPA, ROAS, CVR
     - Frequency, reach, view rate, VTR
   - Use GA4 + UTMs + Looker Studio to:
     - Track sessions, conversions, revenue
     - Build or at least edit a simple performance dashboard

3. **Test & Learn Mindset**
   - Design simple A/B tests:
     - Creative A vs B
     - Landing page A vs B
     - Bid strategy A vs B
   - Keep a **“Test Log”** with hypothesis → setup → results → learning.

### Expected Outcome (End of Month 6)

- Able to set up and optimise full-funnel campaigns on Meta & Google with minimal supervision.
- Can explain performance results to a client in clear, simple language.
- Ready for **Performance Specialist / Junior–Mid level** responsibilities.

---

## Phase 2 – Strategy, Segmentation & Funnel Thinking (Months 6–12)

**Main goal:** Move from “good executor” to someone who can **design funnel-based media strategies**.

### Focus Areas

1. **Audience & Segmentation**
   - Build personas and map them to:
     - Prospecting vs remarketing vs loyalty segments
   - Use:
     - Custom & lookalike audiences
     - Interest, behaviour, and intent-based segments
   - Understand first-party data usage:
     - CRM list uploads
     - Exclusion lists
     - Value-based audiences (high LTV, repeat buyers)

2. **Full-Funnel Planning**
   - For a given brand, define:
     - Awareness KPIs (reach, frequency, VTR, ad recall proxies)
     - Consideration KPIs (CTR, engaged sessions, add-to-cart)
     - Conversion KPIs (CPA, ROAS, LTV)
   - Design channel mixes that cover:
     - Upper funnel (YouTube, broad Meta, programmatic)
     - Mid funnel (remarketing, consideration ads)
     - Lower funnel (PMax, branded search, strong DPA)

3. **Creative x Performance Bridge**
   - Translate data into creative briefs:
     - “Hook” angles, messaging, offers
     - Different creatives for TOFU / MOFU / BOFU
   - Use Helin’s storytelling and content background to:
     - Propose new angles
     - Work closely with creative teams

### Expected Outcome (End of Month 12)

- Can co-own **channel strategy** for small/medium accounts.
- Writes clear media plans and test plans.
- Seen internally as a “**go-to person**” for full-funnel performance ideas.

---

## Phase 3 – Growth, Attribution & Consulting Mindset (Months 12–24)

**Main goal:** Grow into a **Senior Performance / Growth Specialist** who connects media to business outcomes (revenue, LTV, profitability).

### Focus Areas

1. **Lead Gen & CRM-Linked Performance**
   - Understand lead stages:
     - Raw lead → MQL → SQL → Customer
   - Work with CRM data to:
     - Import offline conversions
     - Optimise for qualified leads, not just cheap leads
   - Set up:
     - Lead quality dashboards
     - Simple cohort & LTV analyses with analytics / Excel

2. **Attribution & Budget Reallocation Logic**
   - Learn the basics of:
     - Multi-touch attribution logic (even if models are handled by tools)
     - Contribution vs last-click comparisons
   - Build simple budget reallocation frameworks:
     - If CPA/ROAS deviates X% from target, shift Y% of budget
     - Use test-and-learn to justify shifts

3. **Growth & Experimentation Owner**
   - Define a **North Star Metric** for each account (e.g. qualified leads per week, revenue, LTV).
   - Plan monthly growth experiments:
     - New channel tests
     - New offer or funnel tests
     - Creative “big swings”
   - Document learnings and convert them into playbooks.

4. **Mini-Consultant & Client-Facing Skills**
   - Present performance to clients in a story form:
     - Context → Data → Insight → Action
   - Proactively propose:
     - New creative ideas
     - New funnel structures
     - New measurement approaches

### Expected Outcome (End of Month 24)

- Able to act as **account performance owner** for important clients.
- Comfortable leading QBR-style (quarterly business review) presentations.
- Ready for titles like:
  - **Senior Performance Marketing Specialist**
  - **Growth Specialist**
  - In time, **Performance Lead / Growth Lead**

## Suggested Rituals

### Weekly

- 30–60 min: campaign performance deep dive
- 30 min: write insights + next steps
- 30 min: learning block (course, article, case study)

### Monthly

- 1 **major experiment** (new test)
- 1 **self-review** against roadmap
- Update this repo with:
  - `/notes/YYYY-MM-progress.md`

### Quarterly

- Refresh goals for the next 3 months.
- Re-align with mentor / manager on expectations.
